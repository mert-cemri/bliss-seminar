General Information

Berkeley Laboratory of Information and System Sciences is a research center in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science Department, University of California, Berkeley (UCB). People in BLISS focussed on problems in information and coding theory, optimization, statistics, control and communications. This website is currently under construction.

BLISS currently comprises 6 main faculty, 14 students and 5 postdocs. We all sit in 264 Cory, a large lab on the second floor of Cory Hall.
